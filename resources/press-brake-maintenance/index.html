Key buying checks

  • Confirm material, thickness, bending length, and tonnage.
  • Compare supplier factory capability, certifications, inspection workflow, and service support.
  • Ask for controller options, tooling compatibility, video inspection, packaging, and delivery report.

FAQ

How do I choose press brake tonnage?

Start from material, thickness, bending length, die opening, and bend geometry, then confirm with the supplier's engineering team.

Why buy from a press brake factory?

A factory can align customization, quality control, spare parts, and service support in one accountable workflow.
