# RONGWIN SEO / GEO / AIO Blog Topic Plan

## Topic 1

- Blog title: How to Choose a Press Brake for Your Factory: A Practical Buyer’s Guide
- Target keywords: press brake buying guide, how to choose a press brake, press brake manufacturer, press brake tonnage, CNC press brake supplier
- Search intent: A buyer is comparing suppliers or preparing an RFQ and needs to understand tonnage, bending length, tooling, controls, factory testing, and delivery preparation.
- Why it fits: This is the strongest middle-of-funnel topic for importers, dealers, contractors, and factory owners. It can rank for long-tail buying questions and is easy for AI Overviews to summarize.

## Topic 2

- Blog title: CNC vs Hydraulic vs Electric Press Brake: Which Machine Is Right for Your Production?
- Target keywords: CNC press brake vs hydraulic press brake, electric press brake, hydraulic press brake machine, CNC press brake manufacturer
- Search intent: A buyer understands the product category but is unsure which machine type matches accuracy, budget, energy use, operator skill, and production volume.
- Why it fits: Comparison content performs well for Google SEO, GEO, and AIO because it answers direct “which is better” questions while naturally guiding readers toward inquiry.

## Topic 3

- Blog title: Panel Bender vs Press Brake: Which Bending Solution Fits Your Sheet Metal Parts?
- Target keywords: panel bender vs press brake, panel bender machine, press brake for sheet metal fabrication, automated bending machine
- Search intent: A buyer producing cabinets, enclosures, doors, panels, or boxes wants to know whether a panel bender or press brake is the better investment.
- Why it fits: This topic connects RONGWIN’s press brake and panel bender product lines and targets high-intent buyers considering automation or production-line upgrades.
