FAQ

How do I choose press brake tonnage?

Start with material, thickness, bending length, die opening and bend geometry. RONGWIN can help check your requirements before quotation.

Can RONGWIN customize the machine?

Yes. OEM/ODM customization can cover controller, voltage, tooling, appearance, configuration and project-specific bending needs.

What should I send before requesting a quote?

Please send country, product type, material, thickness, bending length, quantity and any special factory constraints.
